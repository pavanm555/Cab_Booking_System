Our Foresight

We Vitians struggle to find taxis while travelling to campus from the nearby airports or railway station. The taxi and autorickshaw fares are insane and really unfair. We need to find a way to optimise this problem to give feasible result.

So, We have created a website which lets the students avail all the transport facilities at reasonable rates. To optimise the travel charges we have used the current fuel rates and travel distances from major locations and are providing one-way and two-way services based on the client's request.
